# Referral Writing Guidelines

## Good referral language

- Would you feel comfortable referring me?
- I was wondering whether you might be open to referring me.
- I would be grateful if you would consider referring me.
- If you feel my background is relevant, I would greatly appreciate
  your consideration.

## Avoid

- Please refer me.
- I need a referral.
- Can you submit my referral immediately?
- I am the perfect candidate.
- I am uniquely qualified.

## Good evidence language

- My research helped shape...
- I led...
- I partnered with...
- The work contributed to...
- The findings informed...
- The role closely aligns with...

## Recommended greeting

- Hi [Name] — I hope you're doing well!
- Hello [Name], it's great to e-meet you.
- Hi [Name], I hope you're having a good week.

## Recommended closing

- I would be very grateful for your consideration and excited to
  learn more about the opportunity.
- Thank you very much for your time and consideration.
- I completely understand if you're not in a position to refer, and
  I appreciate your time either way.
